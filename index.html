The Los Angeles Lakers

A little background information about The Lakers

The Los Angeles Lakers are a professional basketball team located in Los Angeles, California and are apart of the Western Conference in the NBA. The Lakers have their home games at the Staples Center,which is an arena shared with the Los Angeles Clippers, the Los Angeles Sparks and the Los Angeles Kings of the National Hockey League. The Lakers are one of the most successful teams in the history of the NBA, and have won 16 NBA championships, their last being in 2010. As of 2015, the Lakers are the second most valuable franchise in the NBA according to Forbes, having an estimated value of $2.7 billion.

How it all started....

This franchise started with the purchase of a disbanded team in 1947, the Detroit Gems. The new team began playing in Minneapolis, Minnesota, their new name was the "Minneapolis Lakers" due to their state's nickname, "Land of 10,000 Lakes". Initially a member of the National Basketbal League (NBL), the Lakers won the 1948 NBL championship before joining the Basketball Association of America(BAA) and winning five of the next six BAA and NBA championships in Minneapolis after the NBA formed in 1949. The team was led by their center George Mikan, who is described by the NBA's official website as the league's "first superstar".
