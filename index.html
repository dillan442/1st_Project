Los Angeles Lakers

Who Are The Lakers?

The Los Angeles Lakers are a professional basketball team located in Los Angeles, California and are apart of the Western Conference in the NBA. The Lakers have their home games at the Staples Center,which is an arena shared with the Los Angeles Clippers, the Los Angeles Sparks and the Los Angeles Kings of the National Hockey League. The Lakers are one of the most successful teams in the history of the NBA, and have won 16 NBA championships, their last being in 2010. As of 2015, the Lakers are the second most valuable franchise in the NBA according to Forbes, having an estimated value of $2.7 billion.
